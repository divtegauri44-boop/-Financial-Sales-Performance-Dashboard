# 📊 Financial Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project was developed as part of the Data Analyst Internship - Task 3 (Dashboard Design).

The objective of this project is to create an interactive Power BI dashboard that helps business stakeholders analyze sales performance, profitability, and product performance through data visualization and business insights.

## 🎯 Objective

Design an interactive dashboard for business stakeholders to:

* Monitor sales performance
* Track profitability
* Analyze product performance
* Identify business trends
* Support data-driven decision-making

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* Microsoft Power BI
* Microsoft Excel

## 📂 Dataset

Dataset Used: Financial Sample Dataset

The dataset contains information related to:

* Sales
* Profit
* Units Sold
* Products
* Countries
* Date/Year
* Discounts
* Gross Sales

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard includes the following KPIs:

### 💰 Total Sales

Displays the overall revenue generated.

### 📊 Total Profit

Displays the total profit earned from sales.

### 📦 Total Units Sold

Displays the total quantity of products sold.

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 📈 Sales Trend Over Time

A line chart showing sales performance across different periods.

### 🌍 Country-wise Sales Performance

A bar chart comparing sales performance across countries.

### 🛍️ Sales Distribution by Product

A donut chart displaying the contribution of each product to total sales.

### 🔍 Interactive Filters

Users can filter dashboard results using:

* Country
* Product
* Year

### 💡 Business Insights

A dedicated insights section summarizes key findings from the data.

### 🎯 Recommendations

Provides actionable recommendations based on dashboard analysis.

## 💡 Key Business Insights

* Total Sales reached 118.73M.
* Total Units Sold reached 1.13M.
* United States recorded the highest sales performance.
* Paseo is the top-performing product by sales.
* Sales showed significant growth during late 2014.
* Product sales are concentrated among a few high-performing products.
* Top-performing products drive the majority of overall sales.

## 🎯 Recommendations

* Focus on high-performing products to maximize revenue.
* Increase marketing efforts in top-performing countries.
* Monitor sales trends for better forecasting.
* Improve strategies for lower-performing products.
* Use data-driven insights to support business decisions.
